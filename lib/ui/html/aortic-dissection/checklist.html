Call early in the clinical course - calls for any patient with a potential aortic dissection are welcome. When calling, please provide:

  • Patient Name and DOB
  • Diagnosis: ascending aortic dissection, descending aortic dissection, aortic aneurysm
  • Pertinent co-morbidities - See Patient Risk Factors
  • Current hemodynamic medications, mechanical ventilator settings, and neurologic exam
  • Any signs of malperfusion - See clinical findings
  • ABG, BMP, LFT, CBC, coagulation studies and lactate (drawn within 2 hours of transport)
  • Access lines and monitoring
  • Your facility's ED call back number

MGH Aortic team member will provide:

  • Decision regarding acceptance
  • Patient disposition: Direct to OR vs. ICU vs. Stepdown
  • Any action / investigation recommended for optimization before transport

For acccepted patients:

  • The referring center should contact Boston MedFlight 1-800-233-8998 (preferable) or other critical care transport (CCT) team per your hospital protocol.
  • If intubated, sedation and chemical paralytics recommended for transport
  • Prepare and provide transport team with an adequate quantity of vasoactive drips and other continuous infusions (at least 2-3 times anticipated need for transport)
  • Please report any change in status prior to or during transport to MD Connect at 1-617-726-3384

Images:

  • To facilitate transfer of images so they arrive in advance of the patient, a dedicated link (LifeImage) will be sent to your Radiology Department to upload the images
  • PLEASE ALSO SEND ALL IMAGING STUDIES ON A CD WITH THE PATIENT (even if they are sent electronically via LifeImage)
